The Best Movies According to Jaytee

My top 3 Movies

Dune 2

I love this movie because it's the second adaptation on the books which contain an incredible amount of worldbuilding, politics and depth behind it. its about the main protagonist Paul Atreides uniting the fremen on arrakis to enact revenge on those who destroyed his Family House and embracing the fact that he is apart of a messiac prophecy that is a galaxy spanning holy war for power.

A Silent Voice

I like this movie for the character depth and coming of age story. A silent voice is a Japanese animated coming of age story That focuses on the protagonist; who is a former school bully who, after years builds his own prison of regret becuase of how he had treated others is now tryig to atone for his behavior in the past and trying to forgive himself.

Soul

I love this movie becuase it's about a jazz pianist who gets his soul seperated from his body before a big gig, and ends up tutoring a jaded soul who is more cynical than him to find her "spark" in the meaning of her life. Eventually discovering that the true meaning of life lies in the small moments in everday life.
